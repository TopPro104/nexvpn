## Design Context

### Users
Mix of tech-savvy users (understand protocols, want control) and casual users (just need VPN to work). Used on desktop (Windows/macOS/Linux) and mobile (Android). Context: daily usage, often in background — quick connect, occasional settings tweaks.

### Brand Personality
Modern, approachable, capable. Not hacker/terminal aesthetic. Not corporate/boring. Feels like a well-made tool — Spotify/Discord tier polish with utility focus.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Current dark theme with purple accent is approved — looks good visually
- Priority is UX/usability improvements, NOT visual redesign
- Must NOT feel overloaded or complex — progressive disclosure is key
- Anti-references: corporate VPN clients, overly complex dashboards

### Design Principles
1. **Simple by default, powerful on demand** — beginners see clean interface, advanced features are discoverable but not in-your-face
2. **Information density matches context** — don't waste space, don't overwhelm
3. **Every interaction should feel snappy** — no unnecessary modals, minimal clicks for common tasks
4. **Consistency over decoration** — same patterns everywhere, no one-off layouts
